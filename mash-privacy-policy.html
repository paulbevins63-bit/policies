Privacy Policy — MASH

Last updated: 14 July 2026 · Kentucky Bootleggers Software Inc. · Contact: Paulie.n.SD@protonmail.com

MASH (Modular Adaptive Self-healing Host) is an on-device AI companion from Kentucky Bootleggers Software Inc. Its defining design choice is that the AI runs entirely on your phone. This policy explains what that means for your privacy.

What we collect and send to us: nothing

MASH has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We (the developer) operate no server and never receive your conversations or any other personal information. We cannot read what you type to your companion.

Your conversation stays on your phone

Your chats with the companion, and any facts it remembers about you, are stored in an encrypted database on your device. The AI model that generates replies also runs on your device — your messages are processed locally and are not sent to us or to any AI cloud service.

When MASH connects to the internet

A fresh install of MASH makes no network connections on its own. Two optional features can use the internet, and neither sends your personal data to us:

  • One-time model download. The on-device AI needs a language-model file (about 770 MB). On first run, MASH offers to download it once from a model host. This transfer brings data to your phone; it does not send any information about you. You can decline and use the app without the AI.
  • Optional web search. MASH can fetch fresh information to help answer a question, but this is off by default. It only makes web requests if you explicitly turn on a networked search mode in the app's configuration. When enabled, your search terms are sent to the search service you selected (for example your own private search instance, or a public engine) — never to us.

Working with the Kentucky Spiritual Guide app

If you also install the Kentucky Spiritual Guide, it can ask your MASH companion to reflect on what you're reading. That exchange happens locally between the two apps on the same device and does not leave your phone.

The AI model

MASH's optional on-device model is Meta's Llama 3.2, used under the Llama 3.2 Community License. The model runs locally; using it sends nothing to Meta or to us. This product is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta.

Children

MASH is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Material changes will be reflected in the app's store listing.
